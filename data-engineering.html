Data Engineering

We design and build reliable data foundations that support analytics, reporting, and advanced use cases such as AI and automation.

Our approach

Our work covers the full data lifecycle, from ingestion to consumption. We help teams structure raw data into clean, well-modeled datasets that are performant, cost-efficient, and easy to understand.

We typically work with technologies such as Python, SQL, Spark, Databricks, BigQuery, Snowflake, Azure Data Factory, Airflow, and dbt, depending on the client environment.

Strong emphasis is placed on data quality, monitoring, and operational stability.

Key capabilities

  • Batch and streaming pipelines using tools such as Airflow, ADF, Databricks Jobs, Kafka or Pub/Sub
  • Lakehouse and warehouse modeling in Snowflake, BigQuery, or Delta Lake
  • Medallion architecture and dimensional modeling
  • Data validation using custom checks, dbt tests, or Great Expectations
  • Observability and alerting through logs, metrics, and pipeline monitoring
  • Performance tuning and cost optimization
