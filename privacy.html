PRIVACY POLICY - PERSONAL DATA PROTECTION

1. General Information

SOFTSKILL TECHNOLOGY SRL, as owner and administrator of the website https://softskill.ro, respects the privacy and security of personal data processed by each visitor of this website.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal data, in accordance with Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (GDPR) of the European Parliament and of the Council of 27 April 2016. We process personal data only in safe conditions, strictly for the purposes described below.

SOFTSKILL TECHNOLOGY SRL is committed to maintaining the confidentiality of all personal data provided through:

  • contact forms on the website
  • email communication
  • phone communication

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal data to third parties.

2. What Personal Data We Collect

2.1 Contact Data

When you contact us to request information or an offer, we may collect the following data:

  • First and last name
  • Email address

Providing this information is voluntary but necessary in order for us to respond to your request.

2.2 Device and Technical Data

When you visit our website, certain technical data may be automatically collected, such as:

  • IP address
  • Device type (mobile, tablet, laptop)
  • Operating system
  • Browser type
  • Screen resolution
  • Date and time of access

This information is collected through cookies and analytics tools and is anonymous, meaning it cannot identify you personally. For more details, please consult our Cookies Policy.

3. Methods of Data Collection

Personal data is collected through:

  • Contact forms available on https://softskill.ro
  • Email communication at: info@softskill.ro

4. Purpose of Processing Personal Data

SOFTSKILL TECHNOLOGY SRL processes personal data for the following purposes:

  • responding to inquiries and requests
  • preparing personalized service offers
  • drafting contracts and invoices
  • communicating information related to our services
  • improving website functionality and user experience
  • legal and accounting obligations

Personal data will never be used for purposes incompatible with those listed above.

5. Use of Statistical and Anonymous Data

We may use anonymized or statistical data for:

  • internal reports and analysis
  • service improvement
  • website optimization

This data cannot be used to identify individuals.

6. Disclosure of Personal Data

We do not sell or distribute your personal data. Personal data may be disclosed only in the following situations:

  • when required by law or public authorities
  • to comply with legal obligations
  • to protect the rights, property, or safety of SOFTSKILL TECHNOLOGY SRL
  • to trusted service providers (IT, hosting, analytics, marketing platforms), based on confidentiality agreements

Only the minimum necessary data will be disclosed.

7. Your Rights Under GDPR

According to GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Right to be informed (Articles 13–14)
  • Right of access (Article 15)
  • Right to rectification (Article 16)
  • Right to erasure (“right to be forgotten”) (Article 17)
  • Right to restriction of processing (Article 18)
  • Right to data portability (Article 20)
  • Right to object to processing (Article 21)
  • Right not to be subject to automated decision-making (Article 22)
  • Right to lodge a complaint with the National Supervisory Authority (ANSPDCP)

You may exercise these rights at any time by sending an email to info@softskill.ro

8. Minors

The website https://softskill.ro is not intended for children under the age of 14.

  • Minors under 14 may only interact through their legal representative.
  • Minors over 14 may do so only with parental consent, in accordance with applicable law.

We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors.

9. Data Retention Period

Personal data is stored:

  • for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected
  • or until the user requests deletion or withdrawal of consent

In general, data may be retained for up to 1 year for legal, evidentiary, or archival purposes, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

10. Data Security

SOFTSKILL TECHNOLOGY SRL implements appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect personal data against:

  • unauthorized access
  • alteration
  • disclosure
  • destruction

Data is stored on secure servers and accessed only by authorized personnel.

11. Contact

For any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or the processing of personal data, please contact us at info@softskill.ro
