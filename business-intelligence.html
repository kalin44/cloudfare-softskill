Business Intelligence

We help organizations transform raw data into trusted insights used for daily and strategic decision-making.

Our approach

Our focus is on consistent metrics, clear definitions, and reliable dashboards. Instead of disconnected reports, we build a shared analytical layer that aligns teams across the business.

We commonly work with Power BI, Looker, Looker Studio, Tableau, and Qlik, depending on reporting needs and existing tooling.

Key capabilities

  • Analytics-ready data marts designed for reporting
  • KPI and metric definition aligned with business logic
  • Semantic layer design using dbt, LookML, or BI modeling layers
  • Executive, operational, and product dashboards
  • Self-service analytics enablement for business users
  • Governance and reporting best practices
