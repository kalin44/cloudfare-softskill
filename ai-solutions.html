AI Solutions

We build practical AI solutions that integrate directly into existing systems and workflows.

Our approach

Our focus is on applied AI rather than experimentation. We help teams use large language models and machine learning where they provide clear value, such as internal assistants, document search, and automation.

We typically work with OpenAI, Azure OpenAI, LangChain, vector databases (Pinecone, Weaviate, Chroma), and cloud-native services.

Key capabilities

  • LLM-based applications and internal assistants
  • Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) solutions for documents and knowledge bases
  • Intelligent search and content enrichment
  • Classification and tagging pipelines
  • Evaluation frameworks for accuracy and relevance
  • Integration with data platforms and APIs
